About Us

Solachey is a leading brand in the USA, specializing in modern lighting, illumination solutions, and HVAC supplies. We proudly serve both trade and contract professionals, as well as everyday consumers, delivering high-quality, innovative products that meet diverse needs. We are driven and passionate to meet the customer’s lighting and space conditioning requirements. 

From energy-efficient lighting to advanced HVAC systems, Solachey offers a unique mix of modern solutions, including exclusive pieces that can only be found with us. With a focus on quality and innovation, we are dedicated to inspiring and transforming spaces through superior design and technology.
